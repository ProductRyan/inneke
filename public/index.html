My name is Inneke.

Heritage

My name honors three generations of wonderful women from my mom's family line.

The Belgian spirit of my name comes from my great Bobonne (a Belgian phrase for grandmother), Andrea - who came to North America after WWII.

Ineke (with only one 'n') is a Dutch version for my Bobonne's name, Ingrid.

I share a double 'n' and 'eke' with my mom, Annemieke - who also goes by her diminutive, Mieke (Me-kah).

Pronunciation

Phonetic: In-nah-kah
